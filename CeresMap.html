Places of Interest

Amberridge Caves

Dark, unlit caves through Amberridge following natural caverns carved by the Fuderunt River.

Greatbark Woods

Thick woods teeming with life. The Fuderunt River flows through the forest to Greatbark Lake.

Tauros Valley

A deep ravine carved out in the plains of Ceres, it is a known danger zone for Pokémon stampedes.

Saltstone Quarry

A stone quarry frequently plundered for fossils. Colchite has opened an area away from stonework for trainers to dig.

Lawngreen Meadows and Windfarm

Wind turbines dot these cliffside meadows.

Pokéfarms

Silvervein Lake

Huge lake in the middle of Ceres Plains. It is fed by underground rivers.

Blisterhoof Ranch

Pokeranch that trains Pokemon capable of traversing plains and desert.

Hoofprint Pond

Ruins of Griph

The ruins of an ancient city, destroyed by a past eruption and mostly reclaimed by the desert.

Tealstar Dunes

Seaside beaches and sand dunes used as a get away

Topaz Forest

Spanning half of Ceres, Topaz Forest is full of jungle and forest life. Somewhere in the eastern part ancient tombs are said to be hidden.

Victory Road Cliffside

Cliffs overlooking most of Ceres. A glorious view before the final challenges.

Mount Cuspis

Huge pyramidal peak, long since carved out by glacial ice. This has left most of the inclines incredibly steep.
